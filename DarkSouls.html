Dark Soul's

Dark Soul's is a game released initially for the PS3 and Xbox 360 in 2011 and was later remastered for the PS4 and Xbox One in 2018. It was devloped by the company that also made Armoured Core, FromSoftware and they took a big gamble with the release of Dark Soul's.

Dark Soul's is an action RPG in which the player gets to create a character and choose from a few different classes. While playing, you explore the world, collect different items and fights enemies. As you explore you will encounter different bosses that have a unique attack pattern compaired to a regular enemy. The more bosses and enemies you kill, the more souls you will gain, these are used for buying items and levelling up your character.
