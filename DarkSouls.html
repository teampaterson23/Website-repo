Dark Souls is a game released initially for the PS3 and Xbox 360 in 2011 and was later remastered for the PS4 and Xbox One in 2018. It was devloped by the company that also made Armoured Core, FromSoftware and they took a big gamble with the release of Dark Soul's.

Dark Souls is an action RPG in which the player gets to create a character and choose from a few different classes. While playing, you explore the world, collect different items and fights enemies. As you explore you will encounter different bosses that have a unique attack pattern compaired to a regular enemy. The more bosses and enemies you kill, the more souls you will gain, these are used for buying items and levelling up your character.

Gameplay 8.5/10
The gameplay of Dark Souls is very punishing and encourages the player to wait,
then learn attack patterns of bosses and use the many different weapons they find around the world.
Graphics 6/10
The graphics in Dark Souls are decent, for the time in 2011 they would have been pretty good,
but because the game got a remaster, the graphics got slightly upgraded.
Story 7/10
The story of Dark Souls is very in depth and detailed, the only problem is people have to look for clues and hidden details in character dialogue and item descriptions. For the die hard fans, this is exactly the kind of storytelling they like, but for newcomers to the game, it can be difficult and confusing to understand which might push them away from trying to find out what happens. In the story of the game, you need to ge the lordvessel and fight the Great Lord Gwyn to undo the curse of the Undead.
