There are other games that FromSoft have made other than the Dark Soul's series, these games inlude, Bloodborne which was released in March of 2015, Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice, Demon Soul's and Elden Ring. Each of these games have their own uniqueness, with Dark Soul's being inspired by Demon Soul's, this caused the creation of the Dark Soul's series that we all know today.

Bloodborne

Bloodborne was released for the PS4 in March of 2015 and is similar to Dark Soul's but also has some unique mechanics, one big difference being that Bloodborne is a much more aggressive game than the others, encouraging players to keep attacking after taking damage because they can regain that lost health. There is also transformable weapons, which allows the player to change a weapon mid fight, changing the flow of the attack and how to weapon deals damage. The atmosphere of Bloodborne is very dark and gothic, with the player exploring the city of 'Yharnam' while trying to survive.


Sekiro

Sekiro was released on the 22nd March 2019 and is completely different to the Dark Soul's games. The gameplay focuses on sword attacks, parrying and maintaining a new mechanic called the posture bar. The world of Sekiro is a fantasy version of the Sengoku era in Japan. It features many different locations such as Ashina, The Sunken River and many more the player can explore. The bosses in this game are similar in the way that they fight you, but they require the player to learn attacks and parry at the correct time. There are many different upgrades for the player such as special moves/attacks, gaining more health and more.

Demon Soul's

Demon Souls was the released before Dark Souls and was the game to inspire the creation of it. The orignal Demon Souls was released in 2009 for the PS3 and the remake in November 2020. Demon Souls was a bit different to Dark Souls, this game had a mechanic called Character and World Tendency, the way this worked was anytime the player killed a boss or did a certain action, the Character or World Tendency can change. This can affect the enimes of the game. If a player completes good actions the Tendency will lean towards white, this mean enemies will be weaker but also drop less souls, while the player can do the opposite and complete bad actions, the Tendency will become black, this makes it so that enemies are stronger but also drop more items.
