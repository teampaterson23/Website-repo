Dark Souls 3 is the final game in the Dark Souls trilogy and was realeased in March of 2016. There were a few new additions to this game compared to the others, such as the introduction of new magic items. This added different types of spells such as miracles, pyromancies and sorceries. With these different types of magic, it added much more choice for the player and added more interesting gameplay.

Catagory Score
Gameplay 8.5/10
The gameplay for this game is fairly similar to the others, the developers did make the animations better and smoother than the other games. The bosses in Dark Souls 3 are considered by many to be the best in the seires, they have amazing backstories, pretty cool cutscences and diologue and have challenging moves the player needs to learn.
Graphics 6/10
The graphics in this game are improved on and look pretty good for the time it was released, there are some beautiful areas for the player to explore and fight their way through. There are many different environments that the player can look around such as Lothric Castle, Irithyll of the Boreal Valley and some returning locations from previous games like Anor Londo.
Story 7/10
The story in Dark Souls 3 is engaging to the player but they also have to go out of their way to find out more information. It follows the Ashen One, on their way to relight the Flame and save humanity.
