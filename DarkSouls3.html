Dark Souls 3 is the final game in the Dark Souls trilogy and was realeased in March of 2016. There were a few new additions to this game compared to the others, such as the introduction of new magic items. This added different types of spells such as miracles, pyromancies and sorceries. With these different types of magic, it added much more choice for the player and added more interesting gameplay.

Catagory Score
Gameplay 9/10
Dark Souls 3's gameplay was considered the best in the seires, the combat was smooth and fully fleshed out, all weapons felt useful and that you had the freedom to use whatever you like. The big thing about the gameplay was the bosses and Dark Souls 3 had some of the best in the seires such as The Nameless King, Pontiff Sulyvahn and The Abyss Watchers.
World 8.5/10
The world in Dark Souls 3 is beautiful and also dark and gloomy, both at the same time.
Difficulty 7.5/10
Dark Souls 3 isn't too difficult but is also not too easy. The game gives the player lots of options to aid them on their journey but still leaves you feeling like you could have something better. The game expands of many different things such as magic and the amount of weapon options that the player has.
