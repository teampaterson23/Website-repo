Game Reviews!

This is where I will write my game reviews different games that I have played over the years. I will be talking about the popular Dark Souls series, how those 3 games changed up the formula and what they did good over the years of development.

Dark Souls 1

On this page I review Dark Souls 1. I talk about the gameplay, different mechanics of the game and delve into what made the game good and what they learned and put into later games.

Dark Souls 2

This next page, I go into detail about the next installment in the triolgy, Dark Souls 2. In this game, the developers have improved on different aspects of the gameplay and different mechanics but also made some worse.

Dark Souls 3

After Dark Souls 2, Fromsoftware made the final installment in the series, Dark Souls 3. They made some major improvments and combined everything that they learned over the years to make what many player consider the best one in the series.
